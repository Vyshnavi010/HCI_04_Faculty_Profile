Research Interests

Dr. Neha Sharma's research focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), with a specialization in deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing (NLP). Her work aims to develop intelligent systems that can solve complex real-world problems through data-driven insights.

Her recent projects explore AI-driven applications in healthcare, autonomous systems, and smart cities. She is passionate about building robust AI models that can perform efficiently in dynamic, real-world environments, focusing on fairness and ethics in AI development.

Dr. Sharma is actively engaged in collaborating with researchers from various domains, aiming to push the boundaries of AI/ML research while ensuring that technology remains accessible and beneficial to society.
