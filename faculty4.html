Research Interests

Dr. Emily Carter's research focuses on Operating Systems (OS), system performance, resource management, and distributed systems. Her work involves improving system efficiency, scalability, and security in multi-core and cloud computing environments.

She has worked extensively on file systems, virtualization, and memory management, with applications ranging from high-performance computing (HPC) to embedded systems. Dr. Carter is also interested in studying the interaction between OS-level mechanisms and emerging hardware architectures.

Dr. Carter frequently collaborates with both industry and academia on cutting-edge research, and she is dedicated to mentoring the next generation of OS researchers and practitioners.
