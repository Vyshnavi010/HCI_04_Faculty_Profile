Research Interests

Dr. Catherine Smith's primary research focus is **machine learning** and **artificial intelligence** with applications in **signal processing**, **image recognition**, and **communications systems**. Her work integrates deep learning algorithms with real-time data processing to optimize system performance.

She is particularly interested in **reinforcement learning** and **unsupervised learning** techniques for adapting and improving communication systems in dynamic environments.
