Research Interests

Ms. Deepthi's research focuses on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), with particular emphasis on user-centered design and usability testing. She works on creating intuitive interfaces and systems that improve user interaction and satisfaction.

Her recent projects involve developing mobile applications for improving user experience in education and healthcare sectors. She is also passionate about researching accessibility technologies for individuals with special needs.

Ms. Deepthi actively collaborates with industry professionals and academia to promote research in interactive technologies and their real-world applications. She believes in the power of collaboration to drive innovation in the field of HCI.
